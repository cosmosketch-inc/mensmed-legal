Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

1. Information We Collect

This App collects only the information necessary to operate social media posting functionality, including OAuth access tokens obtained through platform authorization flows (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook). No personal data beyond what is provided by the platform APIs is collected or stored.

2. Use of Information

Collected information is used solely for the purpose of posting content to social media platforms on behalf of authorized users. Access tokens are used exclusively to authenticate API requests.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any personal information with third parties. Data is only transmitted to the respective social media platforms (TikTok, Meta) as required for posting functionality.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect access tokens and other sensitive data. Tokens are stored securely in environment variables and are never exposed publicly or logged.

5. Data Retention

Access tokens are retained only as long as necessary for the App to function. Users may revoke access at any time through their respective social media account settings.

6. Third-Party Platforms

This App interacts with TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for information on how they handle your data:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Meta Privacy Policy

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.
